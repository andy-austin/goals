## Current Reality

### What's Built
- Goal planning wizard with SMART validation
- 3-bucket methodology (Safety, Growth, Dream)
- Timeline visualization
- Drag-and-drop prioritization
- i18n (English/Spanish)
- localStorage persistence

### Why It's Not Valuable Yet

| What Exists | Why It's Not Premium |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Goal planning wizard | Free in Notion, spreadsheets, any todo app |
| SMART validation | Nice UX, but not worth paying for |
| Timeline view | Visual sugar, not differentiated |
| Progress tracking (proposed) | Manual entry = friction = abandonment |
| Educational content | Free everywhere (NerdWallet, Investopedia) |

**Core problem:** Competing with spreadsheets. Spreadsheets are free.

## Three Realistic Paths

### Path A: B2B Tool for Financial Educators

**Target:** Course creators, financial coaches, advisors

**Value prop:** Branded goal-setting tool for clients/students

**Features needed:**
- White-label/custom branding
- Advisor dashboard to see client goals
- PDF exports for planning sessions
- Embed in courses (iframe)

**Pricing:** $50-200/month per advisor or $5/student

**Why it works:** Not competing with free consumer alternatives.

---

### Path B: AI Financial Coach (Subscription)

**Target:** Beginner investors wanting guidance

**Value prop:** Personal AI coach that helps you save

**Features needed:**
- AI reviews goals, gives personalized advice
- Weekly check-in conversations
- Proactive suggestions when behind
- Plaid integration for bank sync

**Pricing:** $5-15/month

**Challenge:** Plaid costs ~$0.25-0.50/user/month at scale.

---
