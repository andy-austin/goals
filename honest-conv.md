# Honest Conversation: Goals App & Passive Income

## The Reality Check

### What The Affiliate Path Requires

| Activity | Time Investment | Fun Factor |
|----------|-----------------|------------|
| Writing 50+ SEO articles | 100+ hours | Low |
| Applying to affiliate programs | 10-20 hours | Low |
| Getting rejected, reapplying | Ongoing | Very Low |
| Waiting 6-12 months for traffic | Patience | Zero |
| Earning first $100 | ~6 months | Brief dopamine |
| Reaching $1K/month | 12-18 months maybe | If ever |

### The Math

To make $1,000/month from affiliates:
- Need ~10,000 monthly visitors
- Need ~100 affiliate clicks
- Need ~10-20 conversions
- At $50-100 per conversion

To get 10K visitors: You're competing with NerdWallet, Bankrate, and hundreds of other SEO-optimized finance sites with 10+ year head starts and full content teams.

## Honest Questions to Ask Yourself

1. **Do you enjoy writing finance content?**
   - If no â†’ this will feel like a second job you hate

2. **Do you have 12+ months of patience with near-zero revenue?**
   - If no â†’ you'll abandon it at month 4

3. **Is this the highest-ROI use of your time?**
   - A freelance gig or side project with paying clients might earn more faster

4. **Are you doing this because you love the product or because you want passive income?**
   - Passive income from affiliates is a myth until you have massive traffic

> "Passive income" is mostly a lie sold by people whose income comes from selling the dream of passive income.

The closest thing to real passive income:
1. **Capital** working for you (investments)

Everything else is just a business with good margins.
